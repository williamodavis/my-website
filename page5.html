Useful Resources

Looking to level up your Magic: The Gathering experience? Explore this curated collection of helpful resources to enhance your deckbuilding, gameplay strategies, and knowledge of the vast Magic universe. Whether you're seeking top-tier decklists, insightful articles, price trackers, or a vibrant community to connect with, you'll find valuable tools to support your journey as a planeswalker.

Websites

  • Scryfall: A powerful card database with advanced search functionality. You can find specific cards, search by keywords, filter by format legality, and much more.

  • MTGGoldfish: Fantastic for metagame analysis (what decks are popular), deck prices, and in-depth strategy guides for various formats.

  • Card Kingdom: A trustworthy retailer for buying singles (individual cards) and a good source for deck inspiration from their vast library.

  • TCGPlayer: Another reliable retailer for buying cards, with articles and other resources for players.

  • Gatherer: The official Magic: The Gathering card database. Great for looking up exact rulings or older cards that might not be on Scryfall.
